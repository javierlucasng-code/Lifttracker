RPE Scale (Rate of Perceived Exertion)

The RPE scale measures how hard you're working. It helps you track effort and manage fatigue based on "Reps in Reserve" (RIR).

10Maximum Effort. Zero reps left in the tank.
9Hard. Could have done 1 more rep.
8Heavy. Could have done 2 more reps.
7Moderate. Could have done 3 more reps.
6Light. Could have done 4+ more reps.
